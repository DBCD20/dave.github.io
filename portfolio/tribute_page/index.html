Dr. Fe Del Mundo

Dr. Fe Del Mundo

The Mother of Philippine Pediatrics

Order of Lakandula
Order of Golden Heart
National Scientist of the Philippines

“ I feel that
if you give the world
the best that you can,
the best will always come back to you. ”

  • The First Woman Admitted as Student at Harvard Medical School
  • Founded the First Pediatric Hospital in the Philippines
  • Established the First Maternal and Child Health Institution in Asia
  • Developed the First Incubator for Philippine setting
  • Pioneered works for infectious diseases
  • Authored articles regarding Dengue, Polio and Measles
  • Authored the first "Textbook of Pediatrics"

“I wish I could still
go out into all nooks
and corners of the country
and bring better care for children. ”

Awards

  • Received the Elizabeth Blackwell Award for "outstanding service to mankind"
  • Received the Ramon Magsaysay Award for outstanding public service
  • Received the 15th International Congress of Pediatrics award as most outstanding pediatrician and humanitarian in 1977
